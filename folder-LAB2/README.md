Summary
This dataset describes performances of students by characterizing how well they do academically (class performance), while also capturing aspects of their study habits, attendance, behavioural characteristics and other factors that might impact their performance in a positive or negative manner. The dataset consists of many characteristics relating to students' behaviours as well as academic characteristics, in addition to the performance variables. The primary purpose of this dataset is to establish models to predict whether students will pass or fail. This is established classified as a classification problem with students' final performance as the target variable. The model will attempt to identify patterns in the dataset that contribute to successful student achievements.
